When work feels heavy

two forces typically pull in opposite directions

Emotional Drag

Second-guessing every detail and feeling stuck.

Goal Pressure

Finish the project brief so the team can start development

Fox & Feather is a simple way to keep both forces moving in the same direction.

Fox Move

List the three core sections the dev team truly needs, draft those first, and send them for a fast sanity check.

Feather Move

Pause, notice the doubt. Give yourself permission to make a first-pass draft that isn't perfect.

Switch between them as often as you like.

The rhythm itself creates momentum.

Creativity
as Resilience

We're Anna Axenov and Orly Boojor, designers who know exactly what it's like to feel stuck. Through our work, we've learned that creativity isn't just about making things it's a tool for perseverance that turns frustration into possibility.

We created Fox & Feather to help you navigate those moments when you lose your way. We provide the method and tools to help you stop hitting the same walls and start finding smarter ways around, under, or over them.
